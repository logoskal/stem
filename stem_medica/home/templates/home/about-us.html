Company Profile

STEM MEDICA is an Ethiopian registered company dedicated to distributing devices, across Ethiopia. We offer high-quality and affordable medical solutions.

About Us

STEM MEDICA እስቲም ሜዲካ was founded in 2023 GC with an initial capital investment of 100,000.00 USD. The founder, Mr. Geremew Zewdie, a biomedical engineer and visionary entrepreneur, is committed to advancing the medical distribution.

Our goal is to work closely with professionals, and key stakeholders to ensure the healthcare system benefits from the latest technologies.

1. General Objective

Our mission is to make medical equipment, supplies, and technologies easily accessible to the Ethiopian market, with a focus on prevention and control of diseases. We are committed to enhancing the healthcare sector with the latest medical solutions.

2. Vision

STEM MEDICA’s vision is to become a renowned and respected supplier of medical equipment and devices. We aim to transition into manufacturing and contribute to the long-term growth of Ethiopia's healthcare market.

3. Mission

Our mission is rooted in the belief that customers are the most valuable assets of the company. We strive to provide excellent service and ensure satisfaction by:

  • Meeting customer demands swiftly and ethically.
  • Providing goods and services in a timely manner.
  • Collaborating to improve the efficiency of the system.
  • Introducing modern technology to meet customer needs.
  • Coordinating with the healthcare and pharmaceutical  ; industries to bridge the supply-demand gap.

4. Values

Our core values revolve around customer satisfaction, loyalty, and excellence. We focus on the following principles to guide our operations:

  • Customer Loyalty
  • Quality products and services
  • Efficiency and reliability
  • Honesty and integrity
  • Respect and civility in interactions
